Dress Code

Elegant Beach Chic

What to Wear

For Her: Flowing sundresses, elegant maxi dresses, or dressy separates in light, breathable fabrics. Think tropical elegance with vibrant colors or soft pastels.

For Him: Linen shirts with dress slacks or khakis. Light-colored suits are welcome but not required. Embrace the island vibe!

Footwear: Beach-friendly options recommended - wedges, dressy sandals, or loafers. You'll be walking on sand and grass.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

When should I arrive in Jamaica?
We recommend arriving at least one day before the wedding (July 11th) to settle in and enjoy the resort. This also gives you a buffer in case of any travel delays.
Do I need a passport?
Yes! All guests will need a valid passport to travel to Jamaica. Make sure your passport is valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel dates.
Are there room blocks available?
Yes! We have special group rates at Sandals Royal Caribbean. Contact the resort and mention "Jane & Joe Wedding" for our special group rates. Book early for the best availability!
What's the weather like in July?
July in Jamaica is warm and tropical, with temperatures in the mid-80s°F (around 29°C). Pack light, breathable clothing, sunscreen, and bring a light jacket for air-conditioned spaces.
Can I bring a plus-one?
Please check your invitation for details. Due to venue capacity, we can only accommodate guests formally invited. If you have questions, please reach out to us directly.
What about gifts?
Your presence is the greatest gift we could ask for! If you'd like to contribute to our future together, we'll have information about our honeymoon fund. No physical gifts please, as we'll be traveling light!
